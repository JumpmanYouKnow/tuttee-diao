About REZ - ONE

Rez-One is a Waterloo based student housing development company which provides luxury student housing at an affordable price. Rez-One believes that how well you live effects how well you do, the reason why Rez-One always provides the best quality service and living space, aimed to redefine `student housing`.

The student oriented differentiates the itself from other student housing, not only is the quality of the living space topping industry standards, but also is the service provided. Rez-One frequently hold `Thank You`parties for its tenants, providing an opportunity for tenants to get together, the building also offer`s a large and well equipped recreational area for tenants to hangout and connect with one another.

The close proximity of Rez-One`s properties to the two main Universities also separates itself with other student housing providers. With Preston and Bridgeport House within 5 minutes walking distance to Laurier University and Blair and Fergus House, 3 minutes walking distance to the University of Waterloo, the close proximity of Rez-One and the Universities makes it one of the top choices that exists in Waterloo region. Each of Rez-One`s luxuries suites can hold up to 5 people, equipped with individual bathrooms, free Wi-fi, fully equipped kitchen and a huge flat screen TV that tenants are able to access as they please. When it comes to safety, Rez-One ensures that its tenants live in a safe environment 24/7. With all Suites controlled through a key fob security system and each individual room through a keypad security system, tenants are guaranteed the safety of both themselves and their properties through a two level security system. "A Home away from Home" a belief held strongly by Rez-One, and is a belief that the company is able to fulfill through dedication and constant improvement.
